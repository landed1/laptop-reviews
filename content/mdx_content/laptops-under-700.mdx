## Best Laptop Under £700

Finding the right laptop that meets your needs and fits your budget can sometimes be challenging. For those looking to spend under £700, the Acer Swift Go 14 stands out as a top contender in 2024.

### Performance

The Acer Swift Go 14 features an Intel Core Ultra 5 115U CPU. This processor is robust enough to handle day-to-day productivity tasks like word processing and web browsing. It's also capable of tackling more demanding applications such as programming and simulations, making it a versatile choice for students and professionals alike.

### Display

This model is equipped with a stunning 2.8k OLED panel. This means whether you're watching movies or engaging in intensive graphic design work, the visual quality is superb. However, you may find that availability of this specific configuration varies, so it's worth checking with retailers.

### Memory and Storage

With 16GB of RAM and a spacious 512GB internal storage, the Acer Swift Go 14 allows for seamless multitasking and ample space for your files and applications. These specs ensure that even if you're working with large datasets or complex software, the laptop won't slow you down.

### Build and Portability

A sturdy metal chassis gives the laptop both a premium look and feel. The keyboard is spacious, and the touchpad large, enhancing usability. There's also a good quality 1080p webcam, ideal for video calls and meetings. Despite its impressive features, the laptop remains lightweight, and boasts an outstanding battery life of around 11 hours under light usage conditions.

### Connectivity

Connectivity options are comprehensive with Wi-Fi 6E, offering faster internet speeds and improved performance. Ports include two USB-C/Thunderbolt 4s, and an HDMI, providing versatility whether you're connecting to an external monitor or transferring files swiftly.

In conclusion, the [Acer Swift Go 14](https://www.rtings.com/laptop/reviews/best/by-price/under-700) is an excellent investment for anyone in the market for a budget-friendly yet high-performing laptop.
